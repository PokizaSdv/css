Answers to Questions

Question-1:
div style=width: 300px; padding: 20px; What is the total width of the box (including padding)?
Answer-1:
300px (width) + 2*20px (padding) = 340px
Question-2:
div style=width: 200px; padding: 30px; border: 10px solid black; What is the total width of the box (including padding and border)?
Answer-2:
200px (width) + 230px (padding) + 210px (border) = 280px
Question-3:
div style="width: 400px; padding: 20px; border: 15px solid black; margin: 25px; What is the total space taken by the box (including padding, border, and margin)?
Answer-3:
400px (width) + 220px (padding) + 215px (border) + 2*25px (margin) = 520px.
Question-4:
div style="width: 350px;" div style="width: 100%; padding: 15px;" What is the total width of the inner box (including padding)?
Answer-4:
350px (width) + 2*15px (padding) = 380px.

Question-7:

Answer-7:
